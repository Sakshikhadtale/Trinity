How To Get Trinity Balaji Registration?

In order to obtain Trinity Balaji Registration you must have following below documents ready:

  • PAN
  • AADHAR
  • Income Tax Return
  • Bank Details
  • Registration Certificate of business entity

Once you have all of the above documents, our professionals can help you register under the Trinity Balaji portal.

Why Product Listing On Trinity Balaji Is Important?

Trinity Balaji is an e-commerce portal where products and services can be sold to government buyers when products and services are listed properly.

You must upload product images, product specifications, price details, delivery terms, inspection, and test reports, etc in order to sell your products on Trinity Balaji portal.

Our professionals can help you list your products and services properly.

Objective Of Trinity Balaji Registration?

The purpose of getting a Trinity Balaji registration is to ensure a smooth and effective procurement process for government offices requirement. Increase the transparency between private sellers and government buyers.

Who is a Primary Buyer on Trinity Balaji?

The following types of Authorized officers can register as Primary User on Trinity Balaji who is equivalent to –

  • Secretary
  • Head of the Office at Sub Center / Unit
  • Branch of a Government Organization
  • A PSU
  • Autonomous Bodies
  • Local Bodies
  • Constitution Bodies
  • Statuary Bodies
